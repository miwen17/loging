Most Famous

Korankei Gorge is famous nationwide for the beauty of its autumn foliage. The red maples burst into fire in the fall, setting the Tomoe River flowing through Asuke-cho aflame. There are said to be about 4,000 maples planted here.Until today there are some 11 varieties of maple thriving here, each with its own distinctive leaf shape and color, weaving a stunningly beautiful tapestry of color.
